# Backlog Chef - Executive Summary

## The Elevator Pitch
**Backlog Chef transforms Scrum refinement meetings into sprint-ready Product Backlog Items with AI-powered quality assurance, risk detection, and stakeholder intelligence.**

Think: *"Grammarly for Agile Requirements"* - but it doesn't just check grammar, it prevents incomplete requirements from entering sprints.

---

## The Problem ($3B Market)

**Every refinement meeting creates 2-3 hours of manual work:**
- Scrum Masters transcribe discussions into tickets
- Missing acceptance criteria discovered mid-sprint
- No historical context ("didn't we discuss this before?")
- Inconsistent quality across teams
- Lost tribal knowledge when people leave

**Real Cost:** 20-30% of sprint capacity wasted on clarification loops.

---

## The Solution

### Not transcription. Intelligence.

**8-Step AI Pipeline:**
1. **Event Detection** - Recognizes meeting type (refinement/planning/retro)
2. **PBI Extraction** - Identifies candidate backlog items from discussions
3. **Confidence Scoring** - Multi-dimensional quality assessment (7 metrics)
4. **Context Enrichment** - Searches similar work, past estimates, lessons learned
5. **Risk Detection** - Flags dependencies, scope creep, missing decisions
6. **Question Generation** - Creates questions WITH proposed answers
7. **Readiness Assessment** - Objective Definition of Ready evaluation
8. **Multi-Format Output** - Publishes to Azure DevOps, Jira, Confluence, Obsidian
