# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Phase 1: MVP (Months 1-3)
- Target: Early adopters, dev tool enthusiasts
- Channels: Product Hunt, HackerNews, LinkedIn
- Pricing: Freemium (10 meetings/mo) + Pro (€49-99/mo)

## Phase 2: Scale (Months 3-6)
- Target: SMB software companies
- Channels: Atlassian partnerships, conferences
- Pricing: Team (€299/mo), Business (€999/mo)

## Phase 3: Enterprise (Months 6-12)
- Target: Large enterprises
- Features: On-prem, SSO, custom integrations
- Pricing: €5k+/month
